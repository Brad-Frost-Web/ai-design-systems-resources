The course's central thesis. Because LLMs can produce things so quickly, it becomes essential to ask whether the output is good — across visual design, UX, and front-end code, but also in the deeper senses of good judgment, good taste, and whether it is good for users, organizations, and the world. The course argues this makes design systems matter more, not less, because they define the bar of quality LLM output must meet.
