Anthropic's LLM, which arrived soon after ChatGPT. The course characterizes its strengths as reasoning, long context windows, and safety, and the instructors are 'big fans' — it features heavily throughout the course. Claude (whether Claude Chat, Claude Code, or Claude Cowork) has deep integrations into the local machine to access your files, plus connectors, skills, and MCPs. At the time of recording it did not directly support raw image generation the way Gemini or ChatGPT do, though it can generate icons via SVG and is strong at handling files. Claude Opus is cited as an example model from the provider Anthropic.
