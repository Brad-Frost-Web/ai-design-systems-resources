A reusable instruction set that teaches an AI how to do a specific, repeatable task, encoding best practices, templates, and domain expertise. A skill is usually a markdown file, sometimes paired with Python or JavaScript tools depending on its purpose. The course's example is a 'create a proposal' skill that knows your brand, colors, format, and tone. The thesis: skills turn general-purpose AI into a specialized collaborator, especially valuable for menial, repetitive, or tedious work.
