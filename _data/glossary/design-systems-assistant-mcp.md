An MCP that FigmaLint runs behind the scenes to surface best-practice recommendations for common components. When auditing the Altitude button, it compared the component against popular design systems such as Carbon and Polaris and suggested options like a size variant, full-width, loading, and icon-only. It also informs FigmaLint's suggested component descriptions by drawing on best practices for common components like buttons.
