The principle that AI needs appropriate, persistent context to be useful; without it, interacting with AI feels like interacting with a goldfish that constantly loses its memory. This principle sets up TJ's context-based design system workflow, where each stage inherits the prior stage's context and feedback loops feed context back to earlier stages.
