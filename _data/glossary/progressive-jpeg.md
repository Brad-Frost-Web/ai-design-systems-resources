The metaphor for how the course itself is being shipped: rather than completing one chapter linearly before the next, the team starts 'blurry' with the gist and core concepts across the whole stack and increases fidelity over time, like a progressive JPEG resolving as data loads. This lets students get the big picture and core demos sooner and leaves room to slot in new and emerging best practices as they are born.
