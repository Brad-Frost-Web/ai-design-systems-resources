Code review is the practice of requesting that specific people put their eyes on your work before it is merged. The course shows requesting a reviewer on a pull request so the change goes through their gate, where they can open the file, see what changed, leave comments, and approve with lingo like "looks good to me" before the merge is confirmed. Teams can require reviews and checks as part of a workflow to ensure quality.
