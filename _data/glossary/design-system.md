After surveying definitions from Salesforce, the Interaction Design Foundation, Nielsen Norman Group, and Figma, Brad describes a design system as critical UI infrastructure and, more memorably, as 'the story of how your organization designs and builds digital interfaces' — a shared narrative and language a team agrees to. He argues that with LLMs now handling many of the mechanical bits, that shared story matters more than ever.
