A broad, varied field whose term was coined at Dartmouth in 1956, building on Alan Turing's 1950 imitation game (the Turing test). The course traces its arc through the AI winters, the 1986 backpropagation paper, AlexNet's 2012 deep-learning breakthrough, the 2017 transformer, and the agentic 2024–25 wave — but clarifies that when this course says 'AI,' it really means large language models.
