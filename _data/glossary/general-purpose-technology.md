Borrowing from Mustafa Suleyman's The Coming Wave, the course describes a general purpose technology as a pervasive innovation that can be applied across many industries and applications, ushering in a new era or paradigm that fundamentally changes how we think, behave, and exist. Examples given include money, agriculture, the written word, steam, semiconductors, the internet, and now AI.
