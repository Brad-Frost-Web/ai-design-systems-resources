In Figma, a reusable design element created from a styled frame (e.g. a button built with auto layout, padding, fill, and stroke). Once made a component, copies are placed as instances, and variants (such as default vs. primary) let designers swap states in the right sidebar — the general gist of how component libraries work in Figma.
