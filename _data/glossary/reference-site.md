A consolidated website that brings a design system's design and development documentation together in one place for all consumers, including non-technical stakeholders. Because Figma and Storybook docs tend to be siloed and overly design- or dev-specific, reference sites (e.g. IBM's Carbon site) gather overview, usage guidelines, style, code, and accessibility docs — teaching what to do and what not to do. Tools like Zero Height, Supernova, and the newer AI-incorporating Mintlify make building them easier.
