From Alan Turing's 1950 paper that begins 'Can machines think?' — the test asks whether a human judge can have a conversation with a human and a non-human entity (a computer) and not be able to distinguish between the two. The course presents it as the philosophical foundation of AI that we experience every time we interact with an AI.
