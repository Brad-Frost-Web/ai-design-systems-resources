The course reframes "handoff" for the AI age: because AI has democratized who can create code, non-developers — especially designers, but also BAs and other stakeholders — can now prototype and create real things. Thanks to Git and the sturdy processes of a Git-powered workflow, those prototypes can be responsibly carried over the line into real feature work that makes its way into production, blessed by the professionals who know how to ship code safely.
