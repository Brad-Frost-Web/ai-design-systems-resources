TJ's workflow concept in which each stage of the design and development life cycle inherits the context from the stage that preceded it, preserving context across the entire lifecycle so that quality is maintained and fewer things slip through the cracks. Feedback loops also feed context back into prior stages, enabling cross-disciplinary collaboration.
