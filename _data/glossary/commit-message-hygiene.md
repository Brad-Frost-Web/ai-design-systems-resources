The course frames good commit messages as a discipline that Brad admits he was historically bad at, often defaulting to vague entries like "work in progress" or "initial commit." It highlights AI as a major help in this department, since AI can generate clear, descriptive commit messages, and shows the practice of committing distinct chunks of work (e.g. "initial AI-generated website") so the project history is meaningful and readable.
