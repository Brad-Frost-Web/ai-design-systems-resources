The course's framing of how the pieces fit together: the LLM is the brain that reasons and generates; agents are the hands that take action and carry out tasks; subagents let the system parallelize and delegate; MCPs connect to your real external tools and data in real time (the USB-port analogy); and skills teach the AI how to do your specific work well. The whole point of the stack is that an AI understands your context and can act on it — and it's the foundation that powers the AI-assisted design systems work throughout the rest of the course.
