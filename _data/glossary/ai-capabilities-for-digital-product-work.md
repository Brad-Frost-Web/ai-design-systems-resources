The course's five buckets for how AI plugs into digital product work: generate (design, code, images, prototypes, copy, data), iterate (refine, fix, ideate over artifacts), translate (between environments and modalities), analyze (thinking, research, code, design, content), and plan/execute (break work into actionable steps and run them autonomously or semi-autonomously via agents).
