One of the ingredients of a design system: the agreed-upon rules for 'we do things like this here' — e.g. which colors, typography, and other choices the organization uses and which it does not. The course notes standards are living, breathing things that constantly change, so there is almost always a gap between the ideals in a standard and real product reality.
