The course's set of interconnected guiding principles that frame every decision the instructors make when wielding AI and design systems together: humanity, safety, intentionality, responsibility, nuance, quality, accessibility, foundations, context, collaboration, curiosity, multiplicity, practicality, and durability. The course encourages teams to run the same exercise and articulate their own shared values.
