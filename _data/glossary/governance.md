Maintaining and governing a design system across a multifaceted, multi-layered ecosystem — deciding what goes where, when and whether changes happen, and whether those changes are intentional. The course lists it among the persistent hard parts of design systems, alongside versioning, breaking changes, adoption, and contribution.
