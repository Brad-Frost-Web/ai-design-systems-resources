The principle of holding non-binary, critically considered views about AI, rising above the polarized hype cycle. The course invokes Melvin Kranzberg's first law that 'technology is neither good nor bad, nor is it neutral,' stressing that one solution is rarely the solution and that your context should determine the right approach.
