The idea that AI and design systems unlock a truly connected relationship in which the design system informs and influences what gets created with its ingredients, while the resulting products in turn inform and influence what lives in the design system. These are living, breathing systems that learn and grow from their usage and feed that learning back into the continued design and development cycle.
