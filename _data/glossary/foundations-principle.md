Described as the crux of the entire course: AI is powerful but can make a huge mess, so the goal is to marry the generative, expansive nature of AI with the thoughtful, sturdy foundations of an organization's design system. Building upon these foundations and establishing tight feedback loops is how you achieve quality at scale.
