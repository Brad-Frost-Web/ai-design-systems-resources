The course's framing for AI's real value: AI acts like a highlighter marker that exposes pain points teams used to accept as inevitable — slow, tedious, manual, repetitive workflows, siloed teams, narrow perspectives, and feedback loops that never make it back to the start of the process. Beyond the 'boring' upside of speed and automation, the deeper value is broadening perspectives, augmenting work, helping teams learn from each other, and closing the feedback loop.
