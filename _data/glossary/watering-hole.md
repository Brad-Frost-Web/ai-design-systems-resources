The course's metaphor for design systems as the shared gathering place where otherwise siloed groups (design, development, product, and non-technical stakeholders) come together and speak the same language for how they design and build UI at an organization. It frames the design system as the connective tissue that keeps aligned the team effort required to design, build, launch, maintain, and grow digital products.
