The architecture described in Google's 2017 paper 'Attention is all you need.' Where earlier models read text one word at a time in sequence, a transformer looks at every word at once and figures out which word relates to which in parallel — a breakthrough in the speed and scale of training that lets models connect ideas across long passages. The 'T' in GPT stands for Transformer, and all modern LLMs descend from this paper.
